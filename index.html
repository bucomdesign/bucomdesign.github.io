Graduate Courses: Product Strategy & User Experience

Part of the Master of Science in Human-Centered Design program, these courses teach the holistic product-development cycle to an interdisciplinary student body.

CM7XX • Human-Centered Design 1 (UX)

Coming Fall 2026

Guides students through the end-to-end solution development process using a strategic, human-centered approach. Students conduct user research, synthesize insights, frame problems, and generate and validate design concepts.

Project: UX Research & Product Case Study
