Elevator Pitch

Echo + Form explores how technology can transform pottery into a deeply personal, interactive experience. By integrating personal data—such as memories, emotions, and gestures—into the creation of ceramic vessels, this project turns objects into storytelling artifacts that hold emotional weight over time. It merges craft, technology, and emotional reflection to create meaningful, interactive objects that resonate on both a personal and collective level.

Key Elements

  • How emotions and memories shape objects’ meaning
  • The role of interactive technology in craft and storytelling
  • Pottery as a medium for personal and cultural identity
