Taught by Prof. Jen Jiun Choi

This course introduces design strategy and creative thinking as essential tools for students in mass communication, public relations, and advertising; whether or not they pursue careers in design. Through a mix of theory, hands-on practice, and real-world projects, students will learn to approach challenges with a creative mindset, collaborate effectively, and apply design principles to communicate ideas clearly and engage audiences across print, digital, and multimedia platforms.

Design Foundation: Understand core design principles and apply them strategically in communication
Visual Communication: Translate ideas into compelling visuals across print, digital, and multimedia formats
Strategic Thinking: Align design decisions with audience needs, messaging goals, and brand objectives
Creative Software Literacy: Confidently use beginner-friendly tools such as Adobe Creative Apps and Figma
Collaboration & Workflow: Work effectively in teams and manage creative projects from concept to delivery
Storytelling & Presentation Skills: Present, explain, and justify design concepts persuasively
