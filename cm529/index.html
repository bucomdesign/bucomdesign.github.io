COM CM529 • Design Strategy & Software 2

Taught byProf. Jen Jiun Choi

Building on the fundamentals from Design Strategy & Software 1, this course expands students’ skills into motion and interactivity. Students learn the core principles of moving images, including timing, pacing, and visual continuity, and apply static design concepts to dynamic environments. Through hands-on projects, students explore how movement, transitions, and interactive elements can guide attention, convey meaning, and strengthen visual communication across digital platforms. The course also introduces screen-based design considerations, emphasizing clarity, hierarchy, and engagement in time-based and interactive formats.

Syllabus Class Drive Figma Adobe Creative Cloud

Time-Based Design

Dynamic Visual Communication

Design Continuity

Interaction Design Thinking

Advanced Creative Software Skills

Conceptual Storytelling

Use motion and interaction to strengthen narrative, pacing, and emotional impact.
